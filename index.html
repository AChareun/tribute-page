Antonio Gramsci

Philosopher, Politician, Journalist, Sociologist, Marxist

A picture of Antonio Gramsci

Here's a timeline with some of Gramsci's life milestones

  • 1891 - Born in Ales, Sardinia
  • 1911 - Won a scholarship to study at the University of Turin
  • 1913 - Joined the Italian Socialist Party
  • 1915 - Abandoned his education due to financial and health problems
  • 1916-1920 - Worked as Journalist and Editor on a variety of newspapers and started educating and organizing workers
  • 1921 - The PCI was founded with Gramsci as a leader and representative
  • 1924 - As head of the PCI, Gramsci got elected as deputy for the Veneto
  • 1926 - Got arrested by the fascist police, despite his parliamentary immunity
  • 1937 - At the age of 46, and after his health deteriorated due to many years of prison, Antonio Gramsci died

"For twenty years we must stop this brain from functioning" said his prosecutor at trial. Gramsci kept writing from prison despite all his health problems.
